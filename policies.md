# Bookly Support Policies

## Shipping

Standard shipping usually takes 3-5 business days.

Express shipping usually takes 1-2 business days.

Orders over $35 qualify for free standard shipping.

## Returns

Books can be returned within 30 days of delivery.

Books must be unused or damaged on arrival to qualify for an automatic return.

Damaged items qualify for a refund to the original payment method.

## Refunds

Refunds are sent to the original payment method.

Most refunds take 3-7 business days after the return is approved.

Orders outside the 30-day return window need a teammate to review them.
